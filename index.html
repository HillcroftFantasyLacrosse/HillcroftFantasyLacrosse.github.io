Selection

Each team consists of 10 players (no substitutes) with the following restrictions:

  • 1 Goalkeeper (GK)
  • 3 Defenders (DF)
  • 3 Midfielders (MF)
  • 3 Attackers (FW)

Draft

Your draft must take place before 1st October 2016. On each round every manager will choose a single player to join their team. The order will be randomly generated and will alternate on each round. For example, if there are 3 teams denoted A, B, C, the order will be: A-B-C-C-B-A ... The draft is completed as soon as all teams have 10 players. At the end of the draft, final teams must be sent to Tom Paton.

Transfers

A manager can exchange one of their players for an unselected player on a first-come-first-served basis. There are no restrictions on the number of transfers allowed. Teams must comply with the selection restrictions at all times. Transfers can be submitted at any time after the draft is completed, and are immediately binding.

Points

Points are awarded to or deducted from players for the following actions. These points are assigned to the team owning the player at the time of the action. Positions are determined by the initial season starting position, not playing position in a given match.
